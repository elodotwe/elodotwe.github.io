Recordings of homilies from various parishes

In preparation for Sunday Mass, I have wanted to hear prior years' homilies to help me unpack what's in the readings. A lot of bad came out of the COVID-19 pandemic, but one good thing is that many parishes started to livestream their Masses in 2020 and have continued since--giving us a robust repository of Mass (and therefore homily) recordings to use.

Note I am a layperson with no specific permission to do this; below are only links to publicly available streams.
